Abstract

This study introduces a novel methodology for reconstructing high-resolution CT images from two X-ray images and explores its application in spine imaging. Our approach leverages advanced deep learning techniques to generate high-quality, diagnostic CT scans while minimizing patient exposure to radiation. The results demonstrate significant improvements in accuracy and clinical applicability.
