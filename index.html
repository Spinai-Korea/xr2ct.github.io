Abstract

XR2CT is an advanced 2D-to-3D spinal CT reconstruction framework that leverages only two orthogonal X-ray inputs to generate high-resolution (256×256) CT volumes in near–real-time. By combining style transfer to harmonize diverse clinical X-rays with standardized synthetic data and an EfficientNetV2-based encoder, XR2CT accurately recovers subtle spinal anatomy while substantially reducing radiation exposure.

This innovation provides high-fidelity reconstructions that support precise neurosurgical interventions, offering a promising new path toward safer, faster, and more reliable spinal imaging.
